1) Choose your meeting details

Start by selecting a name for your meeting and choosing dates that work for you. You can select multiple dates to give your participants options to choose from. This flexibility helps ensure you find a time that works for everyone.

2) Share with participants

Send a link to your meeting to other participants. They can indicate their availability by selecting either "Yes" or "If needed" for each proposed date. This helps you understand who can definitely attend and who has flexibility in their schedule.

3) Select the best time and meet

From the three most popular dates chosen by participants, select the optimal time that works for everyone. Once you've made your decision, you're all set to proceed with your meeting. The app makes it easy to see which dates have the most availability.
